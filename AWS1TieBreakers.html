MLG AW Season 1 Tiebreakers 02/06/15

After further review, in the case that TK loses both games including a 3-0 to aR and aR wins their other match, there will be a 3-way tie for the final spot at the record 20-24 with those two teams and Rise Nation. According to MLG's rules, in the presence of a 3-way tie head to head matchup win % between all 3 teams in the tie will determine the tiebreaker.
With these previous results occurring the head to head match win % would look like the following:
TK 5-3 (63%)
Rise 4-4 (50%)
AR 3-5 (38%)
This would leave TK to win the tiebreak and qualify for the playoffs. As a result, TK has secured their spot in the Season 1.
